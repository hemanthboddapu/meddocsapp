## â“ FAQ

**How many backups are kept?**
The last 5 backups. Older ones are automatically deleted.

**Do backups work without internet?**
Yes! Everything is local, no internet needed.

**Can I backup to SD card?**
Copy the MedDocsBackups folder manually to your SD card.

**What if I lose my device?**
If you shared/uploaded backups, restore them on a new device. Otherwise, data is lost.

**Is this HIPAA compliant?**
The app stores data locally. You're responsible for device security per your institution's policies.
